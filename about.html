Our Story

Chase x Records was founded in 2018 by producer and audio engineer Chase Williams. What started as a small home studio has grown into a premier music production facility serving artists worldwide.

Our philosophy is simple: great music starts with understanding the artist's vision. We believe in creating a collaborative environment where creativity can flourish without technical limitations.
